# Customer B Requirements

B-REQ-004
The system shall measure customer service availability and produce monthly SLA reports per tenant.

B-REQ-005
The platform shall provide REST APIs for service provisioning, terminal inventory, SLA reporting, and alarm retrieval.

B-REQ-009
The system shall retain billing-relevant usage records for at least 24 months.

B-REQ-014
The system shall support software update campaigns grouped by terminal model, region, and service class.
